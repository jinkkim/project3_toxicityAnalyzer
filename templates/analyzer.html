Toxicity Analyzer is a machine learning powered service that provides sophisticated natural language processing over raw text to determine how much toxic the comment is. We trained 1.8 million text data with different machine learning algorithms and adopted XGBoost model as a best fit. There are five categories of toxicity which indicates Angel, Generous, Slightly Toxic, Moderate Toxic, and Severe Toxic. We recommend you to use this to detect toxicity level of your comments in advance and have a good conversation online.


Try our demo now by typing a sentence. You will see how much toxic your comment is. It may take a few seconds.
