Before social media, conversations are something done personally and within a group. We have to identify ourselves and address them directly to other parties. With the growth of social media, we recognize that online conversation become more open and freeing to everyone because of the aspect of anonymity. People can even be carried away when the topic is something they are passionate about and leaving behind toxic comments. This project is about online comments classification. The goal of this project is to build a machine learning model that is capable of detecting different types of toxic comments.Toxicity is defined as anythingrude, disrespectful or otherwise likely to make someone leave a discussion. This model will be helpful to recognize toxic comments in online discussion and discourage people from posting them, so discussion can become more productive.
